What we offer

InKnowledge: the platform to manage knowledge. Multi-featured knowledge base.
Single co-working space where the knowledge over your entire company is accumulated.

This is the place where any employee or customer can promptly retrieve an answer to any issue related to business processes, company activities, or company products.

It will
  • Manage, store, and handle documents in any format.
  • Enjoy advanced search: a database of synonyms; auto-corrected errors / typos; autosuggest of your search phrase; auto-ignoring when the keyboard language is different; quick answers (a full answer under the search box); search by filters and tags, and search within a chosen subject.
Pre-customized templates: omnichannel templates that take into account the communication channels; templates to generate articles or spaces for projects
  • User-friendly UI, widget-based interface composer.
