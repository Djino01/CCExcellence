What we offer

Our robot will save your agents' time and your resources. It will call your customers, deliver the information you need, and conduct a survey or poll automatically
It will
  • Substantially cut your call center expenses without dropping the service quality.
  • Call the customers from your database of tens or hundreds of thousands of people within a short timeframe.
Schedule multiple call campaigns with fixed dates, time, databases, number of calls per person, and intervals between such calls.
  • Deliver detailed information on the status of each call and record relevant information during a call.
  • Strictly adhere to your scripts and avoid human errors.

Pros

Can be trained to support any industry
The robot can be trained to recognize any dialects, colloquial words, expressions, and professional slang for any industry. The robot can also be individually adjusted to fit your business environment. You can fine-tune it yourself as we supply all the required tools.
Service scripts of any complexity
Your analysts can set up scripts as the robot offers all the required tools. No qualified experts are needed to customize scripts. With ready-to-use models and service scripts for multiple industries supplied, you can implement our solution as fast as possible.
Natural flow in dialogs
Can follow the changing subject of a conversation flexibly without losing the context. The robot will not ask for the same information if it has already been provided, but it will ask for additional details, if required, to make sure the customer is understood correctly.
Integration with Speech Analytics
Identifies bottlenecks and optimizes service scripts.
Statistics
Built-in reporting will display the accuracy of recognition, automation percentage, how calls are distributed by subjects, etc.
Voice Biometrics
Offers voice biometrics capabilities to identify customers and verify identity.
If cloud services are not an option
Can be deployed in the cloud or completely on-premises. Works 24/7 without breaks and vacations.
