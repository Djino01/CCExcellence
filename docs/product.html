Virtual Assistants

If you need to

Handle call center peak loads, e.g., avoid website malfunction, without hiring a considerable number of new permanent staff
Automate responses to typical customer calls
Cut your call center agents' payroll expenses by 30–70%
Consult 24/7 but have no agents on night shifts
Cut customer waiting time in queues and cut your 8-800? expenses
Simplify a complicated button-based IVR
Smartly redirect customers to relevant agent skill groups depending on their issue automatically

What we offer

A Virtual Assistants is the best way to handle routine calls and frequently asked questions
It will
  • Answer a call after the first ring, professionally advise on typical issues, or redirect a call to a skilled professional if needed
  • Give details on your new products and make a personalized offer
Communicate in the omnichannel mode: phone, chat, and messenger. No breaks or vacations
  • Recognize a customer issue and ask for additional details if required
  • Provide relevant information immediately, unlike human agents who need some time to find it
  • Consult and handle customer requests automatically, such as adding a service or making an appointment
  • Transform speech to text and record calls automatically for your CRM, analytics module, or other systems
  • Make automated outbound calls, related to such issues as debt recovery, updates of the regulatory database, etc

Pros

Can be trained to support any industry
VA can be trained to recognize any dialects, colloquial words, expressions, and professional slang for any industry. VA can also be individually adjusted to fit your business environment. You can fine-tune VA yourself as we supply all the required tools.
Service scripts of any complexity
Your analysts can customize the scripts as VA offers all required tools. No qualified experts are required. With ready-to-use models and service scripts for multiple industries supplied, you can implement our solution as fast as possible.
Natural flow in dialogs
VA can follow the changing subject of a conversation flexibly without losing the context. VA will not ask for the same information again, if it has already been provided, but VA will ask for additional details, if required, to understand the customer correctly.
Integration with Speech Analytics
Identifies bottlenecks and optimizes service scripts.
Statistics
Built-in reporting will display the level of recognition accuracy, automation percentage, how calls are distributed by subjects, etc.
Voice Biometrics
VA offers voice biometrics capabilities to identify customers and verify the identity
If cloud services are not an option
VA can be deployed in the cloud or completely on-premises
VA works without breaks and vacations
